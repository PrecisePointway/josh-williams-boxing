The Process

Four Rounds. No Shortcuts.

From your first session to fight night. Here's how it works with Josh.

01
Assess

Josh evaluates where you are — fitness level, experience, goals. Whether you've never thrown a jab or you're prepping for your next bout, everyone starts with an honest assessment.

No ego. No judgement. Just an honest look at where you are and where you want to be.

02
Train

Your programme is built around your level — technique drills, pad work, conditioning, strength. Everything structured with purpose, nothing wasted.

Every session has a reason. You'll know why you're doing what you're doing.

03
Fight

When you're ready — and only when you're ready — you step up. Controlled sparring, technical rounds, and eventually competition if that's your path.

Not everyone wants to compete. That's fine. But if you do, Josh has been there under the lights.

04
Win

Whether that's your first 5K without stopping, your first sparring session, or hand raised at Flow Fight Club — winning looks different for everyone. Josh gets you there.

The discipline you build in the gym follows you everywhere. That's the real win.

The Fighter Behind the Coaching

From the Valleys to the Ring

Not a fitness influencer. A fighter who's been through it and come out swinging.

Josh Williams grew up in the Rhondda Valleys — a place that builds tough people whether they like it or not. He found boxing when a lot of lads his age were finding trouble. The gym gave him structure. The ring gave him purpose.

Now a professional boxer competing at Flow Fight Club and beyond, Josh fights for more than belts. He fights to show that where you come from doesn't decide where you end up. His jersey carries Combat Parkinsons and Fighting Homelessness because the causes matter as much as the wins.

He coaches because he remembers what it felt like to have nobody in your corner. Now he makes sure nobody else has to feel that.

Fighting For More

It's Bigger Than Boxing

Josh doesn't just fight for himself. Every time he steps in the ring, he carries these causes on his back.

🧠
Combat Parkinsons

Using the discipline and community of combat sports to support those living with Parkinson's disease. Movement is medicine. Boxing proves it every day.

🏠

Raising awareness and funds for rough sleepers and those at risk. Because everyone deserves a corner to go back to — not just in the ring.

Is This Right For You?

Honest About Who This Suits

Josh's coaching works brilliantly for some people. Here's the honest version.

✓   This is for you if...
  • You want to learn real boxing — not just hit a bag to music
  • You're willing to work hard and be coached honestly
  • You want to get fit with purpose, not just sweat for the sake of it
  • You're a complete beginner and want proper guidance from day one
  • You're an experienced fighter looking for elite preparation
  • You believe tough times build tough people
✗   This probably isn't for you if...
  • You want a soft, no-pressure environment with no accountability
  • You're looking for a trendy fitness class with good lighting for selfies
  • You expect results without putting the work in
  • You can't take honest feedback about your technique
  • You need medical clearance for a serious condition (see your GP first)
