Built In The Trenches

Josh "Hammer Hands" Williams didn't learn to fight in a comfortable gym with air conditioning and protein shakes. He learned on the streets, in the ring, and through years of relentless discipline that most people can't even imagine.

Competing across FFC (Flow Fight Club) and bare-knuckle circuits, Josh has built a reputation for devastating power and an iron will that refuses to break. His fighting style is direct, aggressive, and effective — the same principles he brings to every training session.

Now he's opening his corner to you. Whether you want to get fit, learn to fight, or prepare for competition, Josh brings the same intensity and dedication to your training that he brings to every bout.

Causes We Champion

Every fight has a purpose beyond the ring.

🥊

Combat Parkinsons

Boxing has been scientifically proven to help manage Parkinson's symptoms. Josh proudly supports Combat Parkinsons, using the sport he loves to help those fighting a different kind of battle. A portion of every programme goes directly to this cause.

🏠

Fighting Homelessness

The fight game teaches you that everyone deserves a fair shot. Josh actively supports Fighting Homelessness, working to ensure that the discipline, structure, and community that boxing provides can reach those who need it most.
