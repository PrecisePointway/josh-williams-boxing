Your Weekly Structure

How a Fight Ready Week Looks

01
Technique

Focused technical session — offensive combinations, defensive patterns, counter-punching. Drilled on pads and bags with Josh correcting in real time.

02
Sparring

Controlled technical sparring. Specific objectives each round — work the jab, cut the ring, counter only. Not a war. A learning environment with purpose.

03
S&C

Boxing-specific strength and conditioning. Explosive power, grip endurance, core stability, cardio that doesn't quit in round six. Built for the ring.

04
Review

Video breakdown of your sparring rounds. Josh shows you what you're doing right and what needs work. The camera sees everything your ego misses.
