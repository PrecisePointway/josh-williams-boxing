A Typical Session

What Actually Happens

01
Warm Up

Boxing-specific warm up. Skipping, shadow boxing, footwork drills. Gets your body ready and your brain switched on.

02
Technique

Josh teaches a specific skill — jab mechanics, defensive head movement, combination work. You drill it until it clicks. Proper coaching, proper feedback.

03
Rounds

Pad work, bag work, partner drills. Timed rounds with rest periods — just like a real fight. This is where the fitness comes from.

04
Finish

Core work, conditioning finisher, cool down. You'll leave knowing you've worked. But more importantly, you'll leave knowing something new.
